What is Percentage?

Percentage is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100. Students commonly use percentages to calculate exam results and academic performance.

Formula

Percentage = (Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100

Examples

  • 450 out of 500 = 90%
  • 387 out of 500 = 77.4%
  • 50 out of 100 = 50%

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate percentage?

Divide obtained marks by total marks and multiply by 100.

Can I use this for school exams?

Yes. It works for any exam or test.
